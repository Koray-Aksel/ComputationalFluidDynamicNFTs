Inspired by the people of ancient and of new, we want to add beauty, hope and passion to the web3 space. We aim to provide a foundation for this through our stunning, fully custom, hand-crafted PFPs available with each burnt Access Pass. ORIGO ACCESS PASS

The key to Origo's ecosystem is through our Access Pass. Each whitelisted member will be eligible to mint one Pass which can be later burnt in exchange for one character NFT from the 3,000 character collection. There will be 3,000 equally rare Passes with a mint price TBA.

Settling Your Origo NFT

Origo comes with a unique PFP mechanism that allows them to 'settle' without leaving your wallet. As soon as your NFT is settled, it will begin to accrue additional benefits. As a total settling time accumulates you'll see your NFT achieve new tier levels. Levels will reset if an NFT is sold, unsettled or transferred.
